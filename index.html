Featured Articles

Half-mile Record Broken at Selangor AAA Meet

24 July 1937 Morning Tribune Kuala Lumpur

Saroop Singh broke the half-mile record at the Selangor Amateur Athletic Association meeting, knocking off a second from the existing record of 2 mins. 6 4/5 secs.

First Malayan Sikh Ladies Athletic Meet

17 April 1949 Indian Daily Mail Kuala Lumpur

Historic first athletic meet for Malayan Sikh ladies, with Mrs. J. A. Thivy performing the prize distribution ceremony. Saroop Singh contributed a cup to the event.

Sikh Runner's State Record For Half-Mile

15 July 1957 The Straits Times Kuala Lumpur

Saroop Singh set a State record for the half-mile at the Selangor Athletic Association championships, showcasing continued excellence in distance running.
